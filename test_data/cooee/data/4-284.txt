 [...] Hugh was down here on Easter Monday. He and I had hard scraping up to get as much money as would buy 20 store heifers. Our land would carry a good number of cattle now but we cannot stock it; will have to be satisfied with re-letting a portion and stocking part. I am sorry I cannot do my share, towards stocking it owing to all available cash [...]
